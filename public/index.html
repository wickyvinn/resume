## vita ##

I hail from UC Berkeley's Math and English departments (2006-2010), then Hackbright Academy, where I learned how to code properly (Summer 2013).

Nowadays, I spend most of my time writing in Scala for the platform team at Versal.com, an open publishing platform for anyone to create interactive online courses (2013-).

Before that I was a resarch manager at a finance services company called Glass Lewis & Co., where I designed databases and built data visualization tools using Python and MySQL (2010-2013).

I spent a summer in Cairo teaching Southern Sudanese refugee children English and Algebra while their nation eagerly awaited the results of the referendum for seceding. They were super cute, and some essays cataloging my experiences can be found here (Summer 2010).
